Animation NC II & NC III

Master 2D and 3D animation techniques, character design, and visual storytelling. Learn industry-standard software and develop a professional portfolio.

2 Years (Grade 11 & 12)
TESDA NC II & NC III
DepEd SHS Diploma
Apply for This Program

Course Overview

Program Objectives

This program equips students with comprehensive animation skills, from foundational drawing to advanced 3D animation, preparing them for careers in animation studios, game development, and multimedia production.

Target Audience

Creative individuals with interest in drawing, storytelling, and technology. No prior animation experience required - we start from the basics.

Industry Relevance

With the growing animation industry in the Philippines and globally, skilled animators are in high demand for films, TV series, games, and digital content.

Major Subjects

Fundamentals of Drawing

Develop essential drawing skills including perspective, anatomy, form, and composition through traditional and digital media.

120 hours Grade 11

2D Animation Principles

Master the 12 principles of animation, character design, storyboarding, and traditional 2D animation techniques.

160 hours Grade 11

3D Modeling & Animation

Learn 3D modeling, texturing, rigging, and animation using industry-standard software like Blender and Autodesk Maya.

200 hours Grade 12

Visual Effects & Compositing

Create stunning visual effects, motion graphics, and composite various elements into cohesive scenes using After Effects.

120 hours Grade 12

Filipino Animation History

Study Philippine animation heritage and incorporate local cultural elements into contemporary animation projects.

80 hours Grade 11

Portfolio Development

Create a professional animation portfolio showcasing your best work for job applications and further studies.

100 hours Grade 12

What You Will Learn

2D Animation

Traditional and digital 2D animation techniques, character design, and storyboarding

3D Modeling & Animation

3D character modeling, environment design, rigging, and animation

Visual Effects

Compositing, motion graphics, and special effects for film and television

Filipino Visual Storytelling

Incorporating Philippine culture, folklore, and visual traditions into animation
