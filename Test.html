About PIXEL Academy of Bulan

We are the first and only Senior High School Technical Vocational Institution in Sorsogon specializing in digital arts and technology education.

Creating the Next Generation of Digital Innovators

PIXEL Academy of Bulan is a DepEd-recognized Senior High School Technical Vocational Institution dedicated to developing world-class talent in Animation, Game Development, Robotics, and Interactive Media. Our programs combine technical excellence with cultural relevance.

As a TESDA-accredited institution, we offer students the unique opportunity to earn both Senior High School diplomas and National Certifications (NC II/III) in high-demand digital fields.

Our Technical Vocational Courses

Comprehensive Senior High School programs with TESDA certifications in high-demand digital fields

Animation NC II & NC III

Master 2D and 3D animation techniques, character design, and visual storytelling. Learn industry-standard software like Adobe Animate, Blender, and Maya.

2 Years NC II/III

Game Development

Learn to design, develop, and publish games for multiple platforms using Unity and Unreal Engine. Includes programming, design, and project management.

2 Years Certificate

Robotics & Automation NC II

Explore robotics design, programming, and automation systems. Work with Arduino, Raspberry Pi, and industrial automation equipment.

Digital Illustration & Design

Develop your unique artistic style and master digital painting, character design, and visual storytelling using Photoshop, Illustrator, and Procreate.

2 Years NC II
